Our History

The Da Jhong Story

To meet the economic prosperity, rapid population growth, and swift community development of the Danan urban-planning district in Bade — and to relieve the mounting enrollment pressure on the nearby Dacheng and Dayong elementary schools — the Taiwan Provincial Government approved the founding of the school by official letter on 27 June 1992, appointing Mr. Zhong Huang-hua as the first principal. The school formally began enrolling students in the 1994 school year.

On 1 February 1997, Principal Du Sheng-xiong took charge of school affairs. He actively shaped a fine learning environment, guided all teachers into professional development and innovation in materials and methods, and worked to raise teaching quality and effectiveness — encouraging students, through focused dedication, to grow into courteous, orderly, tidy, and diligent young people of their time.

Our Philosophy

The Education We Believe In

Our Benchmark

To nurture warm, kind, respectful, thrifty, and diligent citizens, full of vitality.

Our Goals

To make the school a joyful place where children grow; to provide the finest facilities for the most effective learning; to help students become poised and graceful; and to make the school a stronghold of community culture.

How We Teach

Method: Lively teaching in every day, every subject, and every class, with diverse integrated activities across subjects.

Principle: Sound, well-rounded teaching, so children’s growth and learning advance day by day.

Our Role — What We Believe

Our role is not merely to teach knowledge, but to teach how to live. Good education rests on good teachers; may each give their best and show their best, together nurturing the young saplings of our nation.

School Vision

Truth · Goodness · Beauty · Joy

Our role is not merely to impart knowledge, but to shape character — together with every child, nurturing the next generation.

Emblem Meaning

Hands · Book · Heart

Diligent study seeks truth, busy hands do good, and a loving heart embraces beauty — the pursuit of Da Jhong’s “Truth, Goodness, Beauty.” Reading sharpens wisdom, hands practice kindness, and confidence brings courage, cultivating the spirit of “wisdom, benevolence, and courage.”

With refined education as the center and the hidden curriculum as the radius, Da Jhong’s teachers design a humane learning haven that develops the five domains in balance: moral, intellectual, physical, social, and aesthetic.

Signature Course

Penmanship · Daily Practice

Penmanship is at the heart of our language education. We want children to recognize and write a large vocabulary during their elementary years, and to build good handwriting habits that serve them for life.

Our teachers develop their own integrated penmanship materials for grades 1 through 6, teaching regular, small-seal, and semi-cursive scripts step by step within Mandarin lessons to build a solid, well-rounded foundation. Each semester we hold a calligraphy exhibition so every child’s effort is seen.

Location & Campus

A Well-Connected City Campus

The school borders the Danan base and Dacheng Junior High to the east, linked by Zhongcheng Street and Jieshou Road — Daxi to one side, Taoyuan to the other, and Heping Road to the south connecting to the Danan interchange of National Freeway No. 2 — making it easy to reach.
